Pizza!

Description

Ahhhh Pizza... the classic Italian dish. Here's how to make it:

Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Tomato
  • Oil
  • Salt
  • Mozzarella
  • Basil

Steps

  1. Prepare the dough
  2. Add tomato, Mozzarella, Oil and Basil, with a pinch of salt
  3. Cook in wood oven for a minute and a half
